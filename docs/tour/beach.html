Must-Visit Beaches in La Union for a Fun Weekend Escape

1. San Juan La Union Beach

San Juan La Union Beach is a popular beach destination because of the resorts and restaurants fit for the ‘gram and of course, the surfing! The waves here are so strong that surfers would go all the miles for.

How to get there: Going to San Juan La Union Beach is easy. From Cubao or Pasay, take the Partas bus going to San Juan directly, and alight at Sebay Surf Central in Urbiztondo.

2. Bauang Beach

Bauang La Union Beach lets you enjoy a great day with its long stretch of fine cream sand and crystal clear waters inviting you to swim all day. This is the best beach in La Union to surf, beach bum, and party all night. Best of both worlds, right? You can also explore its rich marine life. While Bauang La Union Beach offers loads of fun, it is also a peaceful beach as not too many people visit here.

How to get there: From Manila, take a bus going to San Fernando City, La Union. Tell the driver or conductor to drop you off the nearest terminal going to Bauang Beach. From there, ride a jeep going to Bauang Beach.

3. Urbiztondo Beach

Because of the Instagrammable cafes and San Juan La Union beach resorts located here, Urbiztondo Beach is one of the most famous and touristy La Union beaches. It’s also perfect for surfing and if you’re a newbie, there are many surfing schools around the corner.

How to get there: From Manila or Cubao, ride a bus bound for La Union and tell the conductor you are going to Urbiztondo Beach in San Juan La Union so they can drop you off near the place.
